Here at WaterPal we have strived to create a stress-free application that is extremely user friendly and easy to use. Although we have IT support available in our business, our goal is to see that our customers have a hassle free experience. The idea behind our application is to help the citizens of Ireland easily plan out and monitor their by-yearly charges. Our developers have worked hard to make the WaterPal app as user friendly as possible, and easy to navigate.

Here is a list of the different services we provide:


- Breakdown Of Water Charges Per Household

- Total Charges For Specific Houses Per Annum

- Free Downloadable App

- Website With IT Support And Backup

- Water Meter Installations

- Extensive IT and customer support
