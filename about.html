Welcome to waterpal industries, Meet the team. Here at Waterpal we strive for excellence in service and its products but behind the great service is a lot of great people. Ranging from the It Support and customer service people upstairs or the skilled installers who you trust to do a good job. The idea came about after we first heard of the water taxes, there was a lot of talk of water meters and allowances etc but nothing about how to monitor how much water you use, so the four co-founders, Aaron Healy, Leon Mulvaney, Alex Russell and Keith Ferguson came up with Waterpal.

It took a while to get it going and all together, but eventually the pieces fell into place and when people began to hear of our products and the amazing price we were soon busy at work. The main appeal seemed to have been the variety of meters and ways to monitor them such as the user friendly app for ios and android phones or the website which gives you detailed analysis of the water usage over the days. As well as the text message reminder which alert customers to when there is a lot of water being used or when allowances are running low.

We strive to achieve the highest standards within our business, may it be Application development all the way to IT Support.
